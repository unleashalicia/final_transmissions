Instructions

Open the map to find the location of the marker to unlock the next event.

Only ghost hunt if the weather permits. Low light and poor weather conditions can distort the cards, which are necessary to unlock spirits. Always be aware of surrounding dangers when interacting with the game.

To start, grab your cards, put on headphones, open game webpage, login, and confirm to begin or continue a story.

Not sure where to begin? Pick a story from the library and 'go!'.

Ghost Finder

To locate your spirit, flip the switch on the ghost finder.

The knob has three ranges; long, mid and close. You can turn ('click') the knob to help gauge the meter. Continue to adjust the knob based on proximity to the spirit.

Meter

Once you're in the experience you'll see the meter. The meter is displayed in portrait mode only.

Use the meter to guide you towards your next location. As you approach the spirit, you will begin to hear music. When your narrator begins to speak, stop and listen.

Augmented Reality

Once you are guided to your spirit, your phone will switch to camera mode. Augmented reality will display in landscape mode only.

The narrator will notify you when to use a card. Place your marker on a flat surface on the ground protected from wind. Using a card skips forward to the next event.

Make sure your ghost meter is ready

No great ghost hunter allows their equipment to fail in the middle of a supernatural encounter. To ensure that you’re ready to meet some ghosts, check that your meter (phone) is equipped with the following specifications:

  • Running Android (Ghosts have trouble with iOS)
  • Access to your phone’s camera
  • An up-to-date version of Chrome or Firefox

Only ghost hunt if the weather permits

While typical ghosts tend to lurk around in the darkness, our ghosts prefer nicer weather. Search for stories and encounters during daylight (non-eclipse) hours, preferably outdoors in areas without wind, rain, snow, or otherwise inclement weather.

To fully immerse yourself in our stories, we highly recommend using headphones while searching for and experiencing encounters.

Join an experience at your own risk!

If you’re wearing headphones and looking at your phone, be sure to also look out for traffic, be wary of where you’re walking, and every other safety precaution. We don’t need another ghost in this app.

Finding an Encounter

Get to the scene

  • Each story takes place in its own unique setting, so in order to experience it, you have to start in that physical location.
  • The game map on each story page can be used to give you an idea of where ghostly activity can be accessed.

When you are getting close to an encounter, you will hear music.

When you get within close range of an encounter and hear your spectral narrator, you’ve found your encounter. Stop walking and listen.

If you continue walking, you may move out of range you won’t be able to hear your story anymore.

Experiencing an Encounter

When instructed to do so by your narrator, place the marker card associated with the story that you want to view flat on the ground

Remember: Follow the directions of your narrator closely

Only put down your card and check it in the viewer when your narrator instructs you to.
