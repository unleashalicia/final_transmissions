Inspiration

Final Transmissions is an immersive, storytelling experience developed by a team of 6 software engineers in Irvine, California. Inspired by Sleep No More in New York City and the advances of augmented reality in recent years, Final Transmissions uses 3-dimensional sound and music, augmented reality, and geolocation to bring whimsical scenes to real-world settings.
